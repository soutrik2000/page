Synopsis:

Birdman Or The Unexpected Virtue Of Ignorance is a black-comedy-drama which tells us the story of an old superhero portraying actor named Riggan Thompson(Michael Keaton) who desires to start a Broadway Play on his own.

Throughout the movie,he is seen to fight his ego and hallucinating 'BIRDMAN' -a comic-book superhero character. It is later revealed that he used to star in the superhero franchaise featuring birdman which gained him fame and appreciation.The movie takes is through a journey,emotions,identity,self-esteem and brings up a bunch of bitter questions before our eyes.
